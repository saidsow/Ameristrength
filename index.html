Iron sharpens Iron

Our mission is freedom through fitness. At Ameristrength Fitness we want more than just a client/trainer relationship. We want to build rapport and trust in order to achieve the best results possible in and out of the gym. Whether it’s semi-private training, our personalized one-on-one coaching, or through nutritional guidance we empower lives, create accountability, and promote wellness that spans far beyond the four walls of a gym.

Meet the Founder

Hi, my name is Cris McCullen and I’m the founder of Ameristrength Fitness. As a kid, sports and physical activity were my life. I started getting into resistance training when I was 16 and fell in love with powerlifting, bodybuilding, and nutrition. In fact, I loved it so much that I went to school for it and got my bachelor’s in exercise science. I wanted to pursue physical therapy but the family took precedence. I switched careers and became a firefighter/paramedic which I enjoyed but was not my passion. However, it gave me the priceless experience to become my grandmother’s in a home nurse for the last 7 months of her life. After her death I knew I had to get back to what god put me on this earth to do and these are the pillars for which Ameristrength Fitness was built upon.
